Sate Chicken

home

Preparation: 15 minutes
Marinating: 30 minutes
Cooking: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • chicken breast fillets, cut into small pieces
  • skewers
  • Marinade:
    • peanut oil
    • soy sauce
    • lemon juice
    • garlic, finely chopped
    • chilli flakes
  • rice, to serve
  • peanut sauce, to serve
  • tomato and cucumber salad, to serve

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the peanut oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, garlic and chilli flakes
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and marinate for 30 minutes
  3. Thread the chicken pieces on the skewers
  4. Barbecue the chicken skewers
  5. Serve with rice, peanut sauce and salad

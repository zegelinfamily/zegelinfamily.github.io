Scones

home

Source: Muriel Halsted (Country Women’s Association)

Ingredients:

  • 5 cups self raising flour
  • 300ml of cold cream
  • 300ml of lemonade
  • milk
  • salt

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C
  2. Sift flour 3 times adding a pinch of salt while sifting
  3. Fold in cold cream
  4. Add lemonade
  5. Keep folding the mixture together until the flour is all mixed in
  6. Put onto a floured board, cut into desired sizes and brush with milk
  7. Pop them into the oven for ten minutes or until golden brown, turning the tray once during cooking
  8. Enjoy with butter, jam, cream or whatever you like

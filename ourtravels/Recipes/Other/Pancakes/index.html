Pancakes

home

Source: Dad Schepers Leave mixture to stand for 10 minutes before cooking

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups self raising flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 dessertspoon sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • milk, enough to make a thin consistency
  • 2 dessertspoons butter

Preparation:

  1. Beat all the ingredients, except for the butter, together
  2. Melt the butter in a crepe pan and pour into the mixture
  3. Beat the mixture again and let it stand for 10 minutes
  4. Cook pancakes in a dry crepe pan on medium heat

Mediterranean Chicken Bake

home

Serves: 4
Preparation: 15 minutes
Cooking: 50 minutes
Source: Decor website

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 700g chicken breast fillets, cut into large chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 400g can diced tomatoes
  • 2 red capsicums, deseeded and roughly chopped
  • ½ cup deseeded Kalamata olives, roughly chopped
  • ½ cup drained sundried tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 2 tablespoons mixed dried herbs
  • ¼ cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • ¾ cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • green salad and crusty bread, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. In a 3 litre baking dish, combine all ingredients except the feta and mix well
  3. Cover the baking dish with foil
  4. Bake in the oven for about 50 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and tender, and the sauce has thickened slightly
  5. Divide the chicken bake onto 4 plates and top with crumbled feta cheese
  6. Serve with crusty bread and a green salad

Chicken Cacciatore

home

If anchovies are too strong, soak in a little milk for 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg skinless chicken pieces
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • Â½ cup chicken stock
  • 400g can peeled tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon finely sliced basil leaves
  • 2 anchovy fillets, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons black olives
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • pasta or mashed potatoes, to serve
  • vegetables, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a small saucepan and brown chicken pieces on all sides. Transfer chicken to a plate
  2. Stir onion and garlic into saucepan and cook for about 5 minutes
  3. Add wine and vinegar and bring to boil before adding stock and crushed tomatoes with their juice
  4. Transfer chicken, sauce and basil to a baking dish. Season with a little salt and pepper, cover with a lid and cook in a moderate oven for about 35 minutes
  5. One minute before serving, stir in anchovies, olives and parsley
  6. Serve with pasta or mashed potatoes and vegetables

Chicken and Asparagus Risotto

home

Add small amounts of extra water if rice is drying out

Ingredients:

  • 40g butter
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 clove of garlic, crushed
  • 750ml vegetable stock or chicken stock
  • 1 full cup arborio rice
  • ½ cup white wine
  • 1 Chicken breast, baked.
  • ½ bunch asparagus, ends trimmed
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • Small quantity soy sauce
  • grated parmesan cheese

Preparation:

  1. Bake chicken in oven, flavoring with soy sauce and thyme. Set aside to cool. Slice thinly
  2. Cut asparagus to 4 cm lengths and precook in microwave until nearly done. Replace water with cold water to prevent further cooking and preserve color
  3. Heat oil and butter in a large pot and fry onion until golden
  4. Add the garlic and cook for a minute or two
  5. Turn temperature up fairly high and add risotto, striring until grains are coated
  6. Lower temperature and add stock, wine and salt and pepper
  7. Simmer, stiring regularly for about 20 minutes or until rice is nearly done
  8. When Risotto is nearly done add asparagus and cook for a few minutes more
  9. Add most of the chicken to reheat, stirring gently
  10. Serve risotto into bowls. Top with the rest of the chicken and parmesan cheese

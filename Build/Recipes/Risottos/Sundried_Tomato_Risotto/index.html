Sundried Tomato Risotto

home

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, crushed
  • 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 45g butter
  • 1 litre vegetable stock or chicken stock
  • 1 full cup arborio rice
  • ½ cup sundried tomatoes
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • grated parmesan cheese

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a saucepan and fry onion until golden
  2. Add the garlic and cook for a minute or two
  3. Turn temperature up fairly high and add risotto, striring until grains are coated
  4. Lower temperature and add stock, tomatoes, sugar, salt and pepper
  5. Simmer, stiring regularly for about 20 minutes or until rice is cooked
  6. Add small amounts of extra water if rice is drying out
  7. Add half the sundried tomatoes about 5 minutes before ready
  8. Serve risotto into bowls. Top with the rest of the sundried tomatoes and parmesan cheese

Baked Chicken with Lemon, Potato and Green Olives

home

Serves: 2
Source: Bill Granger

Ingredients:

  • 2 large potatoes, cut into large pieces
  • 1 red onion, cut into segments
  • ¾ cup green olives
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 50g pancetta, cut into strips
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 2 medium chicken breasts, cut into large pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • cornflour
  • flat-leaf parsley, to garnish

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Half cook the potatoes in the microwave
  3. Place the potatoes, onion, olives and lemon in a baking dish and pour over the stock
  4. Place the chicken pieces on top, cover with pancetta strips, drizzle with olive oil and season with ground black pepper
  5. Roast for 45 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through
  6. Turn on the griller for a few minutes to brown the potatoes and onion. If the chicken looks like it is ready it can be removed first
  7. Before serving, thicken the sauce with a little cornflour mixed with water
  8. Serve garnished with parsley

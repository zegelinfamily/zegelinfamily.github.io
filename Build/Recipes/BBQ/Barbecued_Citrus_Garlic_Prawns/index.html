Barbecued Citrus Garlic Prawns

home

Serves: 2
Preparation: 15 minutes
Marinating: 1 hour
Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 10 large prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ½ tbsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ½ orange, juiced
  • 1 tbsp white wine
  • ½ tsp dried basil (or fresh to taste)
  • rice, to serve

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the olive oil, mustard, garlic, lemon juice, orange juice, basil and white wine
  2. Add the prawns and stir to coat
  3. Cover and let marinate for 1 hour
  4. Heat BBQ to high heat
  5. Grill prawns for 3 to 5 minutes, turning once, until cooked

Penne With Eggplant Caponata

home

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 small eggplant (about 500g), cut into 2cm cubes
  • 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon caster sugar
  • ½ cup pitted green olives
  • ¼ cup drained capers
  • 2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 200g+ penne
  • freshly grated parmesan, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion gently cook, stirring, for 5 minutes or until light golden
  3. Add chopped garlic and cook for a few more minutes
  4. Increase heat to high, add eggplant and cook, stirring, for 2-3 minutes until golden
  5. Add tomatoes, sugar and a little salt and pepper
  6. Bring to the boil, then simmer over low heat for 15 minutes or until the eggplant is cooked and sauce thickens
  7. Stir in the olives, capers and parsle
  8. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to packet instructions. Drain well, then toss with sauce and serve with parmesan

Cantonese Chicken

home

Serves: 2
Marinating: 2 hours
Cooking: 40 minutes

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornflour
  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into large pieces
  • rice, to serve
  • vegetables, to serve

Preparation:

  1. In a baking dish, mix together the honey, soy sauce, lemon juice, tomato sauce and cornflour
  2. Place the chicken pieces in the baking dish, coating the chicken well with the sauce
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours to marinate
  4. Bake in the oven at 190°C for 20 minutes
  5. Turn and baste the chicken, then bake for a further 20 minutes
  6. Serve with rice and vegetables

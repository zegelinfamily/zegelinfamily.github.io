Chicken Alla Romana

home

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¼ cup bacon bits
  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into large pieces
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • 1 can whole tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • freshly ground black pepper

Preparation:

  1. Heat the olive oil and fry the bacon bits
  2. Add the chicken breast pieces and brown slightly
  3. Add the garlic and cook for a very short while before adding the wine
  4. Cook for a few minutes and then add the tomatoes, red pepper strips and black pepper
  5. Cook slowly uncovered for about 30 minutes, stirring every now and then to prevent sticking

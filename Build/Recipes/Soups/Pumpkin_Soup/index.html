Pumpkin Soup

home

Serves: 2
Source: Margaret Fulton

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 6 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 medium pumpkin, diced
  • ½ cup cream
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add onion and garlic and cook gently until soft
  2. Add the diced pumpkin and cook for 10 minutes until golden brown
  3. Add the stock and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring often
  4. When pumkin is soft remove from heat and cool slightly. Puree with blender
  5. Stir in the cream. Season with salt and pepper

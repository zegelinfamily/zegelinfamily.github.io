Ingredients:

  • ½ cup burghul (cracked wheat)
  • 12 shallots, finely chopped
  • 3-4 cups flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup mint, finely chopped
  • 2 large firm tomatoes
  • 1 large lemon, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Preparation:

  1. Put the burghul into a bowl, cover with water and soak for about 20 minutes
  2. Drain the burghul through a fine sieve, squeezing out as much water as possible
  3. Return burghul to bowl and add shallots, flat-leaf parsley, mint and tomatoes
  4. Toss with the lemon juice and olive oil
  5. Refrigerate until chilled

Moroccan Sweet Potato, Carrot and Chickpea Soup

home

Serves: 4
Preparation: 20 minutes
Cooking: 45 minutes

Can be frozen for later use

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large brown onion, roughly chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon chilli powder
  • 600g orange sweet potato, peeled, diced
  • 500g carrots, peeled, sliced
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 300g can chickpeas, drained, rinsed
  • ½ small lemon, juiced
  • Turkish bread croutons, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat
  2. Add onion and garlic and cook, stirring often, for 3 minutes
  3. Stir in coriander, cumin and chilli powder and cook, stirring, for 1 minute
  4. Add sweet potato and carrot and cook, stirring often, for 5 minutes
  5. Add stock, cover and bring to the boil
  6. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 20 minutes
  7. Add chickpeas to soup, cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until chickpeas are tender
  8. Blend soup, in batches, until smooth
  9. Return to saucepan over medium-low heat
  10. Season with salt and pepper
  11. Stir in 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  12. Heat, stirring, until hot (do not boil)
  13. Ladle into bowls
  14. Serve topped with Turkish bread croutons and sprinkled with pepper
  15. TURKISH BREAD CROUTONS
    1. Cut Turkish bread loaf in half lengthways and chop into 2cm cubes
    2. Spray lightly all over with olive oil cooking spray
    3. Place on a tray and bake in a 200°C oven for 10 minutes or until golden

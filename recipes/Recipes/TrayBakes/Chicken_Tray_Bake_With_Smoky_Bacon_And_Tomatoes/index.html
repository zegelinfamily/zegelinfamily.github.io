Chicken Tray Bake with Smoky Bacon and Tomatoes

home

Serves: 4
Source: Jamie Oliver

Ingredients:

  • 500g mixed-colour cherry tomatoes
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 4 x 180g skinless chicken breasts
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon sweet smoked paprika
  • olive oil
  • 4 rashers of smoked streaky bacon
  • 1 lemon, quartered
  • a few sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • balsamic vinegar
  • 500g asparagus, to serve
  • 8 baby or chat potatoes, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C
  2. Halve the cherry tomatoes and squash the garlic cloves with the flat side of a knife and place in a baking dish
  3. Lay the chicken breasts on top
  4. Sprinkle over the paprika, oregano and a pinch of sea salt and black pepper
  5. Drape a rasher of bacon over each chicken breast
  6. Add the lemon quarters to the baking dish
  7. Sprinkle over the rosemary leaves and drizzle everything lightly with olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  8. Roast in the oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through
  9. Snap the woody ends off the asparagus stalks and about five minutes before the chicken is ready, steam the asparagus until tender. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and season
  10. Divide up the chicken tray bake and asparagus, then serve with boiled baby or chat potatoes

Leek, Chorizo, Cauliflower and Chicken Bake

home

Serves: 4
Source: Australian Gourmet Traveller

Ingredients:

  • 300gm cauliflower, cut into small florets
  • 200gm chorizo, halved lengthways, coarsely chopped
  • 10 garlic cloves, separated
  • 2 leeks, cut into 3cm rounds
  • ½ lemon, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup oregano (loosely packed)
  • ¼ cup flat-leaf parsley (loosely packed), plus extra to garnish
  • 80ml olive oil
  • 4 chicken breast fillets (about 160gm each)

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C
  2. Combine cauliflower, chorizo, garlic, leeks, lemon, oregano and parsley in a large bowl, drizzle with half the olive oil, season to taste and toss to combine
  3. Transfer to a large baking dish and roast until starting to colour, 10 minutes
  4. Place the 4 chicken breasts on the cauliflower mixture, drizzle with the remaining olive oil and roast until the chicken is cooked through
  5. Garnish with extra parsley and serve

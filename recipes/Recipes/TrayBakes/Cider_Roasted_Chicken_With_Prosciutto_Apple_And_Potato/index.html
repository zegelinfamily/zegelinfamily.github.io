Cider Roasted Chicken with Prosciutto, Apple and Potato

home

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 500g chat potatoes, thickly sliced
  • 6 small brown onions
  • 2 Pink Lady or Granny Smith apples, cut into wedges
  • 8-10 thyme sprigs, plus 1 tablespoon extra leaves
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • 4 chicken breast fillets, cut into large pieces
  • 4 thin slices prosciutto
  • 100ml dry apple cider
  • ⅓ cup chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C
  2. Combine potatoes, onions, apple wedges, thyme sprigs and 60ml olive oil in a baking dish, season well and roast for 10-15 minutes
  3. Season the chicken pieces and scatter with thyme leaves
  4. Wrap each chicken piece with a slice of prosciutto, place on top of the potato mixture in the baking dish and drizzle with the remaining olive oil
  5. Add apple cider and chicken stock to the baking dish and roast for 20-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender
  6. Stir through the cider vinegar and serve

Antipasto Barbecue Chicken

home

Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons mixed dried herbs
  • 2 small chicken breast fillet, cut into medium pieces
  • 1 small eggplant, sliced into rounds
  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced on the diagonal
  • 4 roma tomatoes, halved lengthways
  • Â¼ cup (60ml) balsamic vinegar
  • 100g pitted kalamata olives
  • 100g Greek feta, crumbled
  • 1 small bunch flat-leaf parsley, leaves picked, stems finely chopped
  • ground black pepper

Preparation:

  1. Preheat a barbecue flat plate to high heat
  2. Place the olive oil, garlic, herbs, chicken, eggplant, zucchini and tomatoes in a large bowl and toss until well coated
  3. Using tongs, transfer the chicken and vegetable mixture to the barbecue flat plate
  4. Cook, turning the vegetables occasionally and the chicken only once, for 10 minutes or until the chicken is golden and cooked through
  5. Transfer the chicken and vegetables to a large bowl
  6. Add the remaining ingredients to the chicken and vegetable mixture and toss gently to combine
  7. Season to taste with freshly ground black pepper and serve

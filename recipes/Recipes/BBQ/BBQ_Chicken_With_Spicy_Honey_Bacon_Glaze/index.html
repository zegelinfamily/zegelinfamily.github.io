BBQ Chicken With Spicy Honey Bacon Glaze

home

Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into large pieces
  • ⅛ cup balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ cup olive oil or vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon steak seasoning
  • For the glaze THIS NEEDS ADJUSTING UP _ MAYBE DOUBLE
    • 2 slices bacon, chopped - this is OK
    • ⅓ cup honey - 1/2?
    • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce - 3??
    • ½ tablespoon hot sauce - 1-1/2 - 2?
  • potatoes, to serve
  • green vegetables, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Mix balsamic, oil, brown sugar, and seasoning together in a bowl
  2. Marinate the chicken in this mixture for 30+ minutes
  3. Make the glaze
    1. Fry bacon in a small pot with a bit of oil until crispy
    2. Drain excess bacon fat from pot and add honey, worcestershire and hot sauce
    3. Let bubble over medium heat. Once it is bubbly, add in the chopped bacon.
    4. Stir and cook for 4-5 minutes until it has thickened lightly, then remove from heat.
    5. Make sure glaze cools down so it thickens - too much and it is cold, too little and it is runny
  4. Grill chicken pieces, add some marinade as it cooks
  5. Serve topped with bacon glaze, mashed potato and green vegetables

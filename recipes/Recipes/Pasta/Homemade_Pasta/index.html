Homemade Pasta

home

Serves: 2

Do not add salt!

Ingredients:

  • 100g soft-wheat flour
  • 100g fine semolina flour
  • 2 room temperature eggs
  • Green Pasta
    • 50g spinach, washed, cooked and blended
  • Orange Pasta
    • 50g carrots, peeled, cooked and mashed
  • Red Pasta
    • 1 tsp tomato paste
  • extra flour will be needed while processing the pasta with the machine

Preparation:

  1. Pour the flour mixture into a bowl, then the eggs and water in the middle
  2. Beat the eggs and water with a fork and mix thoroughly with the flour
  3. Add vegetable puree if making colored pasta
  4. Knead the dough with your hands until it is completely smooth and elastic
    • If the dough is too dry, add some water, if it is too soft add some flour
    • To help make the dough smooth, run it through the pasta machine several times
  5. Make a ball with the dough, wrap in plastic and let sit for up to 1 hour
  6. Run pasta through the pasta machine several times, refolding each time
  7. Run pasta through at progressively thinner settings until desired thickness is achieved

Mac and Cheese

home

Serves: 3 x 2
Source: Rick Stein - Road to Mexico

Strain pasta in silicon collander as there is a lot of it

Ingredients:

  • 500g macaroni
  • 100g bacon
  • 100g speck
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 100g butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 100g plain flour
  • 50g Parmesan, grated
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1.2 ltr milk
  • 75ml double cream
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 400g mature cheddar + strongly flavored cheese, grated
  • pinch freshly grated nutmeg
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 slices of bread for bread crumbs

Preparation:

  1. Cook macaroni in salted water until al dente, drain well
  2. Preheat the oven to 180°C and grease large pillivut ovenproof dish
  3. Fry the bacon and speck in a pan with some olive oil until crisp
  4. Cheese Sauce
    1. Pour bacon and speck fat into another pot and stir in the plain flour and cook for 1 minute
    2. Add the mustard, take the pan off the heat and gradually whisk in the milk and cream. Add the bay leaf
    3. Put the pan back over the heat, stirring constantly, until it boils and thickens
    4. Take the pan off the heat again, remove the bay leaf and add the cheddar. Stir until the cheese has melted
    5. Season with lots of black pepper and some nutmeg
  5. Stir macaroni into the cheese sauce and pour the mixture into the ovenproof dish
  6. Mix the breadcrumbs with the Parmesan and scatter over the top
  7. Bake for 25–30 minutes, or until golden-brown and bubbling
  8. Serve immediately

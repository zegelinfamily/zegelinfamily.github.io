Spaghetti Alla Carbonara

home

Serves: 2

An authentic Roman spaghetti alla carbonara.

Ingredients:

  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 40g pecorino, grated and extra for serving
  • 200g spaghetti
  • salt
  • 100g piece guanciale (or streaky bacon), roughly chopped into small strips
  • extra virgin olive oil, only if needed
  • freshly ground black pepper

Preparation:

  1. In a small bowl beat the egg yolks with the pecorino and set aside
  2. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to the boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente
  3. In a large frying pan, cook the guanciale/streaky bacon on medium heat, stir frying until crispy. Add a little olive oil only if the streaky bacon is not fatty enough to produce an adequate amount of oil for the sauce
  4. With tongs add the spaghetti to the pan of guanciale/streaky bacon and mix well, adding more cooking water if necessary
  5. Remove the pan from the heat, stir in the egg mixture
  6. Serve immediately with some grated pecorino and freshly ground black pepper

Linguine With Prawns, Snow Peas And Lemon

home

Serves 6
Source: Peter Doyle

Timing is important. Linguine and prawn mixture need to be ready at the same time

For 2 -> 1/3 of recipe but use more prawns, say 200gm pasta and 12 prawns

Ingredients:

  • 400g dried linguine
  • 24 prawns, shelled and de-veined
  • 1 fresh, medium-hot red chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
  • 30 snow peas
  • ½ cup fresh peas
  • 3 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Lemon Vinagrette
    • 2½ tbsp fresh lemon juice
    • 1 cup olive oil
    • salt and freshed ground pepper
  • 1 tbsp basil leaves, coarsely torn
  • salt and freshed ground pepper

Preparation:

  1. Cook the linguine in a pan of boiling, salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pot at high temperature and add prawns
  3. Cook quickly on each side
  4. Lower heat and add lemon vinagrette, peas, snow peas chilli garlic and herbs
  5. Cook for about a minute
  6. When the linguine is cooked, drain slightly, leaving some water. Add prawn mixture

Chicken Piccata

home

Serves: 4
Source: Margaret Fulton

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breast fillets
  • plain flour, seasoned with salt and pepper
  • 30g unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup white wine
  • juice of 1 large lemon
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped

Preparation:

  1. Trim chicken and slice each fillet through the centre into two pieces. If you like them thinner, beat lightly with a rolling pin between two sheets of plastic wrap
  2. Coat the chicken pieces lightly in seasoned flour, shaking off excess
  3. In a large, heavy frying pan, heat the butter and olive oil over medium-high heat, add the chicken and saute, turning once, for 4 minutes. Transfer to a platter and cover to keep warm
  4. Add wine to the pan and deglaze over high heat, scraping up the pan residue, until the mixture is reduced by half
  5. Add lemon juice, capers and parsley, swirling to combine the mixture
  6. Plate the chicken and pour over the sauce

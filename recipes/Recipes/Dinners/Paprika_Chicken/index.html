Paprika Chicken

home

Serves: 4
Source: Margaret Fulton

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut in to large pieces
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 15g butter
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp sweet or hot paprika
  • ¾ tbsp plain flour
  • ¾ cup chicken stock
  • ½ red capsicum, seeded, cut into thin strips
  • ½ can chopped tomatoes
  • ¼ cup chopped parsley
  • 150ml light sour cream
  • salt and pepper
  • basmati rice, to serve
  • green beans, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Pat the chicken breasts dry with paper towel and season with a little salt
  2. Heat oil and butter in a large heavy-based pan on medium high. Brown chicken on each side. Remove from pan and set aside
  3. Reduce heat to medium and cook onion and garlic for 5 minutes, stirring until softened
  4. Add paprika and flour and stir until combined, taking care not to burn
  5. Add stock and stir until simmering
  6. Return chicken pieces to pan, cook for 10 minutes
  7. Add capsicum and tomato with half of the chopped parsley
  8. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer gently for 10 minutes or until chicken is tender
  9. Stir in sour cream and remaining parsley, season to taste
  10. Serve with basmati rice and green beans

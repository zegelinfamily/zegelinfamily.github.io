Pizza Toppings

home

Ingredients:

  • Vegetarian Pizza
    • fetta cheese, thinly sliced
    • 1 cup baby spinach leaves
    • ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
    • ¼ green or red capsicum, thinly sliced
    • pitted Kalamata olives
    • ½ avocado
  • Prosciutto Pizza
    • prosciutto
    • ½ small zucchini, finely sliced
    • ¼ red capsicum, thinly sliced
    • pitted Kalamata olives
    • fresh basil leaves, add after cooking pizza
  • Hawaiian Pizza
    • ham
    • pineapple
    • pitted Kalamata olives

Preparation:

  1. Place pizza stone in cold oven or BBQ
  2. Preheat oven or BBQ to 225°C
  3. Mix 1 tub of tomato paste, 2 large crushed cloves of garlic and oregano (place half of this in a container and freeze for next pizzas)
  4. Cover 2 large pizza bases with tomato paste mixture
  5. Add shredded mozzarella and tasty cheese
  6. Add toppings
  7. Place pizza on pizza stone in oven or BBQ
  8. Bake for 6-7 minutes

Quiche

home

Source: Peter Russell-Clark

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • ½ cup self rasing flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup cream
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1½ cups grated Tasty and Mozarella Cheese
  • 1½-2 cups chopped assorted filling:
    • ham and red capsicum
    • zucchini, red capsicum and red onion
    • ham and leek
    • ham and corn
    • ham and spinach

Preparation:

  1. Combine eggs, flour, milk, cream and butter in blender, mixing well
  2. Stir in cheese and filling, pour into a 24cm quiche dish
  3. Bake at 180°C for 40 minutes until set

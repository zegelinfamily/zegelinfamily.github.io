Baked Teriyaki Salmon

home

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets, skinned and boned
  • 1 200g packet Passage to Asia Teriyaki Sauce
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • Jasmine rice to serve
  • Asian greens (such as baby bok choy), steamed to serve

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 170°C. Place salmon in a baking dish
  2. Pour stir-fry sauce over salmon. Gently turn salmon to evenly coat with sauce
  3. Cover with foil and bake 20 minutes or until cooked
  4. Top salmon with spring onions
  5. Serve with jasmine rice and steamed Asian greens

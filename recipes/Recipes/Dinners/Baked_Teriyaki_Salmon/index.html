Baked Teriyaki Salmon

home

Serves: 4 Source: Passage to Asia Foods website

Ingredients:

  • 4 200g salmon fillets, skinned and boned
  • 1 200g packet Passage to Asia Teriyaki Sauce
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • rice, to serve
  • Asian greens (such as baby bok choy), to serve

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 170Â°C
  2. Place salmon in a baking dish
  3. Pour Teriyaki sauce over salmon
  4. Gently turn salmon to evenly coat with sauce
  5. Cover baking dish with foil
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until cooked
  7. Top salmon with spring onions
  8. Serve with rice and steamed Asian greens

Chocolate Chip Biscuits

home

Makes about 50 biscuits

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • ½ cup castor sugar
  • ¼ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • l cup semisweet chocolate bits
  • 1 cup one-minute oats
  • ½ cup chopped nuts

Preparation:

Beat together sugars, butter, egg, milk and vanilla in a mixing bowl until light and fuffy Stir together flour, oats, nuts, chocolate bits, salt and bicarbonate of soda Stir flour mixture into creamed mixture and blend well Line baking pan with baking paper and drop cookie mixture from a teaspoon about 5cm apart Bake in a 190°C oven for 8 to 10 minutes Let stand for a few minutes to cool and place on wire rack to cool completely

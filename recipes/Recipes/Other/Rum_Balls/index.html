Rum Balls

home

Ingredients:

  • 200 g dark cooking chocolate
  • ¼ cup cream
  • 30 g butter
  • 50 g basic chocolate cake crumbed
  • 2 tsp rum
  • ½ cup chocolate sprinkles

Preparation:

  1. Place the chocolate in a bowl
  2. Combine the cream and butter in a small pan and stir over low heat until the butter melts and the mixture is just boiling
  3. Pour the hot cream mixture over the chocolate and stir until the chocolate melts and the mixture is smooth
  4. Stir in the cake crumbs and rum
  5. Refrigerate for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, or until just firm enough to handle
  6. Roll heaped teaspoons of the mixture into balls
  7. Spread the chocolate sprinkles on a sheet of greaseproof paper. Carefully roll each rum ball in the sprinkles, then place on tray
  8. Refrigerate for 30 minutes or until firm.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

home

Makes about 50 cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cups plain flour
  • ½ cup castor sugar
  • ¼ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • l cup semisweet chocolate bits
  • 1 cup one-minute oats
  • ½ cup chopped nuts.

Preparation:

Beat together sugars, butter, egg, milk and vanilla in a mixing bowl until light and fuffyStir together flour, oats, nuts, chocolate bits, salt and bicarbonate of sodaStir flour mixture into creamed mixture and blend wellLine baking pan with baking paper and drop cookie mixture from a teaspoon about 5cm apartBake in a 190°C oven 8 to 10 minutesLet stand for a few minutes to cool and place on wire rack to cool completely

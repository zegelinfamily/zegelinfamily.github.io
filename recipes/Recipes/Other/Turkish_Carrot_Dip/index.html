Turkish Carrot Dip

home

Serves: 4-6
Preparation: 10 minutes
Cooking: 10 minutes
Source: SBS Food

Makes about 2 cups Serve with bread, vegetable sticks and other dips as part of a meze

Ingredients:

  • 6-7 carrots, grated
  • ½ tablespoon olive oil
  • 200g Greek yoghurt
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • pinch of salt
  • black olives, to garnish
  • olive oil, to garnish

Preparation:

  1. Cook the grated carrot in a frying pan with ½ tablespoon of olive oil until just softened
  2. Remove the carrot from the frying pan and leave to cool for about 15 minutes
  3. Combine the yoghurt, garlic and salt and mix well
  4. Stir in the cooled carrot
  5. Scoop into a serving bowl
  6. Garnish with black olives and drizzle with a little olive oil

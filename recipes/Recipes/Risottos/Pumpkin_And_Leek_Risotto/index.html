Pumpkin And Leek Risotto

home

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • knob of butter
  • 400gm pumpkin
  • 1 leak, finely sliced
  • 1 full cup arborio rice
  • ½ cup white wine
  • 1 litre vegetable stock
  • freshly grated parmesan, to serve
  • freshly ground black pepper, to serve
  • chopped fresh parsley, to serve

Preparation:

Place pumpkin on large rimmed baking sheet. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper, toss to coat. Roast until tender and beginning to brown, stirring occasionally, about 40 minutes.Cool and chop into bite size pieces

  1. Heat the olive oil and knob of butter in a large pot, add the leaks and saute until lightly browned
  2. Add the bacon and fry until it starts to crisp
  3. Turn temperature up fairly high and add risotto, striring until grains are coatedx. Add wine and stir until absorbed, lower temperature and add stock
  4. Stir well, then reduce the heat and cook for 20 minutes until the rice is almost tender
  5. Stir in the chopped pumkin, add a little salt and pepper and cook for a further 3 minutes until the pumpkin is heated
  6. Serve top with grated parmesan, freshly ground black pepper and parsley

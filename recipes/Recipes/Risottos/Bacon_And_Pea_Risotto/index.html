Bacon and Pea Risotto

home

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • knob of butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 6 rashers bacon, roughly chopped into small pieces
  • 300g arborio rice
  • 1 litre vegetable stock, hot
  • 100g frozen peas
  • freshly grated parmesan, to serve
  • freshly ground black pepper, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Heat the olive oil and knob of butter in a large pot, add the onion and saute until lightly browned
  2. Add the bacon and saute until it starts to crisp
  3. Add the rice and stock and bring to the boil
  4. Stir well, then reduce the heat and cook for 15-20 minutes until the rice is almost tender
  5. Stir in the peas, add a little salt and pepper and cook for a further 3 minutes until the peas are cooked
  6. Serve sprinkled with grated parmesan and freshly ground black pepper

Honey Prawns

home

Serves: 4
Preparation: 10 minutes
Marinating: 30 minutes
Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg green prawns, peeled and deveined, tails intact
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine or dry sherry
  • 3 teaspoon cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 5cm piece ginger, peeled and shredded
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 100g snow peas, trimmed
  • 4 green shallots, cut into 4cm lengths
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
  • rice, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Combine prawns, soy sauce, dry sherry and cornflour
  2. Marinate for 30 minutes
  3. Heat a wok over high heat and when hot add half of the oil and swirl to coat
  4. Cook prawns in batches, removing them as soon as they change colour and then set aside
  5. Heat the remaining oil and stir-fry the ginger and garlic until fragrant
  6. Add the snow peas and shallots and cook for 2 minutes until they turn bright green
  7. Return prawns to the wok with any marinade and honey and toss over heat for 1 minute or until hot
  8. Serve with rice and sprinkled with sesame seeds

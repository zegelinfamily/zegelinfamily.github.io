Tofu with Chilli Jam and Spring Onions

home

Serves: 4
Source: Australian Gourmet Traveller

Ingredients:

  • vegetable oil, for frying
  • 600gm firm tofu, cut into 3cm cubes
  • cornflour, for dusting
  • 4 spring onions, thinly sliced, plus extra to serve
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 2 golden shallots, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon finely grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoons chilli jam
  • 1½ tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon kecap manis
  • 1 teaspoon ground Sichuan pepper
  • steamed Asian greens, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Dust tofu cubes in cornflour, shaking off excess
  2. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat
  3. Fry tofu, turning occasionally, for 3-4 minutes or until golden
  4. Drain tofu on paper towel
  5. Heat a little more oil in wok
  6. Add spring onions, garlic, shallots and ginger and fry for 5-6 minutes or until tender
  7. Stir in chilli jam, soy sauce, kecap manis and pepper
  8. Add tofu and stir to coat with sauce
  9. Serve with steamed Asian greens and scatter with spring onion

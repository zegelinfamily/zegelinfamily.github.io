Thai Chilli Jam Chicken

home

Serves: 4
Preparation: 10 minutes
Cooking: 15 minutes
Source: AYAM website

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 500g chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red capsicum, deseeded and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons AYAM Thai Chilli Jam Paste
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • ½ cup roasted cashew nuts
  • ½ cup Thai basil leaves
  • rice, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Heat half the oil in a wok or large frying pan over medium-high heat
  2. Stir-fry the chicken in batches for 3-4 minutes until golden brown. Remove from heat and cover to keep warm
  3. Add remaining oil and red onion to the wok and cook for 2-3 minutes
  4. Add red capsicum, stir-fry for a further 2 minutes
  5. Return the chicken to the wok, stir in the chilli jam paste, soy sauce and lime juice
  6. Stir until warmed through and coated in the sauce
  7. Garnish with cashew nuts and basil leaves
  8. Serve with rice

Chilli Mango Chicken

home

Serves: 2
Marinating: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 large chicken breast, chopped into small pieces
  • 1 tablespoon cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • ½ tablespoon sesame oil
  • ½ tablespoon sesame oil for wok
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon grated or minced fresh ginger
  • 2 cups snow peas
  • 1 mango, peeled, pitted and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chilli sauce
  • black pepper to taste
  • rice, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Combine the chicken, cornflour, soy sauce and sesame oil in a mixing bowl and marinate for 10 minutes
  2. Heat the sesame oil in a wok over high heat
  3. Add the chicken, along with its marinade, and stir-fry until the chicken begins to brown
  4. Add the onion and cook until the onion is translucent
  5. Add garlic, ginger and snow peas and stir-fry for 1 minute
  6. Add the mango, chilli sauce and black pepper and stir-fry until the chicken is cooked
  7. Serve with rice

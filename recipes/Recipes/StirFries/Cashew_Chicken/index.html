Cashew Chicken

home

Marinating: 30 minutes

A little bit of heat can be added to the sauce by using some sambal oelek (chilli paste) or red pepper flakes.

Ingredients:

  • 700g chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry
  • 2 teaspoons minced, peeled fresh ginger
  • 3½ teaspoons cornflour, divided between marinade and sauce
  • ½ cup chicken or vegetable stock (½ Continental stock pot in ½ cup of boiling water)
  • 2½ tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar (or 1 tablespoon white vinegar and ¼ teaspoon sugar)
  • 2½ teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • ⅔ cup cashews or cashew halves
  • 2 spring onions, white and green parts divided and thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sambal oelek (optional)
  • a pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)
  • rice, to serve
  • bok choy, to serve

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, toss the chicken pieces with the sherry, ginger and 1½ teaspoons cornflour
  2. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes
  3. To make the sauce, in a small bowl whisk together the chicken stock, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, 2 teaspoons cornflour, and sambal oelek or red pepper flakes (if using) and set aside
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a large wok over high heat
  5. Add the chicken and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5 minutes
  6. Add the cashews, garlic and spring onion whites and cook, stirring constantly, until the garlic begins to soften, about 30 seconds
  7. Whisk the sauce again to combine and add to the wok
  8. Cook until the sauce thickens, about 30 seconds
  9. Serve with rice and steamed bok choy
  10. Garnish with the spring onion greens

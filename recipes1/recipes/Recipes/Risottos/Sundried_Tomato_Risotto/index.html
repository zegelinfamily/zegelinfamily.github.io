Sundried Tomato Risotto

home

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 45g butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, crushed
  • 1 full cup arborio rice
  • 1 litre vegetable stock or chicken stock
  • 1 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Â½ cup sundried tomatoes
  • grated parmesan cheese, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a saucepan and fry onion until golden
  2. Add the garlic and cook for a minute or two
  3. Turn temperature up fairly high and add rice, stirring until grains are coated
  4. Lower temperature and add stock, tomatoes, sugar, salt and pepper
  5. Simmer, stirring regularly for about 20 minutes or until rice is cooked
  6. Add small amounts of extra water if rice is drying out
  7. Add half the sundried tomatoes about 5 minutes before serving
  8. Serve risotto topped with remaining sundried tomatoes and grated parmesan cheese

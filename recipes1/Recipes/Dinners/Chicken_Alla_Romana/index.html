Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¼ cup bacon bits
  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into large pieces
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • 1 can whole tomatoes
  • 1 red capsicum, cut into strips
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • potatoes, to serve
  • green beans, to serve

Preparation:

  1. Heat the olive oil and fry the bacon bits
  2. Add the chicken breast pieces and brown slightly
  3. Add the garlic and cook for a very short while before adding the wine
  4. Cook for a few minutes and then add the tomatoes, red capsicum strips and black pepper
  5. Cook slowly uncovered for about 30 minutes, stirring every now and then to prevent sticking
  6. Serve with mashed potatoes and green beans
